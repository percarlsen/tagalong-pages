Tagalong Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to tagalong! We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, profile picture.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you use the app, including your interactions and preferences.
  • Post Information: Information about the posts you create, including content, timestamps, and interactions.
  • Device Data: Information about your device, including IP address, operating system, and browser type.

How We Use Your Data

  • Service Provision: To provide and maintain our app's functionality.
  • Communication: To send you updates, notifications, and support messages.
  • Improvement: To analyze usage data to improve our app's performance and features.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Service Providers: Who assist us in providing the app, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When disclosure is necessary to comply with legal obligations.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk of processing personal data.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: To request access to your personal data.
  • Rectification: To request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: To request deletion of your personal data.
  • Restriction: To request restriction of processing.
  • Objection: To object to data processing.
  • Portability: To request transfer of your data to another service provider.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@tagalong.no.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via email at hello@tagalong.no

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
